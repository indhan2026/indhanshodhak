Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

IndhanShodhak is committed to fair and transparent refund practices. Please read this policy carefully before subscribing.

Cancellation Policy

  • You may cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings.
  • Upon cancellation, your access continues until the end of the current billing period.
  • No charges will be made after cancellation for future billing cycles.

Refund Policy

  • Within 24 hours of purchase: Full refund if service not accessed.
  • After 24 hours: No refund for the current billing period as digital access has been provided.
  • Technical issues: If the service is unavailable for more than 48 continuous hours due to our fault, a pro-rated refund will be provided.

How to Request a Refund

  • Email us at indhanshodhak@gmail.com
  • Subject: "Refund Request — [Your Mobile Number]"
  • Include: Razorpay Payment ID, reason for refund
  • Refunds processed within 5-7 business days to original payment method

Non-Refundable Cases

  • Subscription used for more than 24 hours
  • Account suspended due to violation of Terms & Conditions
  • Change of mind after accessing the service

Contact

For refund queries: indhanshodhak@gmail.com
We aim to respond within 24-48 hours.
