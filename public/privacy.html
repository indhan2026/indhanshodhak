📋 Who We Are

IndhanShodhak (इंधन शोधक) is a community-driven fuel availability tracking platform for India. We help citizens locate nearby petrol pumps with real-time fuel availability information during normal and crisis situations.

📦 Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our services:

  • Account Information: Mobile number, email address, full name (required for registration)
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates — only when you actively use the "Find Nearby Pump" feature
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type (for app performance)
  • Usage Data: Fuel reports submitted, pumps visited, features used
  • Pump Owner Data: Business name, pump address, GPS coordinates, contact details
  • Identity Documents: For pump owners and tier upgrades — Aadhaar card (number + name verification only), Retail Outlet License
  • Community Reports: Fuel availability reports submitted by users

We do not collect Aadhaar biometric data. Document verification is limited to name and number matching only.

🎯 How We Use Your Information

  • To show real-time fuel availability at nearby pumps
  • To verify pump registrations and maintain data accuracy
  • To send OTP verification via email for secure login
  • To award community reporter points and maintain leaderboards
  • To detect and prevent false or fraudulent fuel reports
  • To enable government oversight of fuel distribution patterns
  • To send important app notifications and alerts
  • To improve app performance and fix issues

🔐 How We Protect Your Data

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS encrypted connections
  • Passwords are never stored in plain text
  • Session tokens are stored securely with 90-day expiry
  • Cloudflare protection against unauthorized access and DDoS attacks
  • Location data is used only for pump proximity calculation — never stored permanently
  • Identity documents are reviewed only by authorized verifiers and deleted after verification
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties

📍 Location Data

IndhanShodhak requests location permission to find petrol pumps near you. Specifically:

  • Location is accessed only when you tap "Find Nearby Pumps"
  • We do not track your location in the background
  • Location coordinates are used only to calculate pump distances
  • Your precise location is never stored on our servers
  • You can deny location permission and enter your area manually

🤝 Data Sharing

We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Government Officials: Aggregated, anonymized fuel availability data for crisis management purposes only
  • Authorized Verifiers: Pump registration documents for verification review only
  • Service Providers: Email delivery (Gmail SMTP), payment processing (Razorpay), mapping services (MapMyIndia/Mappls) — bound by their own privacy policies
  • Legal Requirements: If required by Indian law or court order

We never share your personal data with advertisers, data brokers, or marketing companies.

🏆 Community Points & Rewards

IndhanShodhak operates a community reward system. Regarding this system:

  • Points earned are tied to your account and are non-transferable
  • Your username may appear on the public leaderboard (you can opt out in settings)
  • Points can be redeemed for subscription discounts at ₹0.05 per point
  • False report deductions are logged and visible to you in your account history

👶 Children's Privacy

IndhanShodhak is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

✏️ Your Rights

As an IndhanShodhak user, you have the right to:

  • Access all personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of leaderboard visibility
  • Withdraw location permission at any time via device settings
  • Request a copy of your data in portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below. We respond within 7 working days.

🍪 Cookies & Local Storage

  • We use browser local storage to remember your language preference and session
  • No third-party advertising cookies are used
  • Analytics are collected anonymously for app improvement only
  • You can clear local storage via your browser settings at any time
