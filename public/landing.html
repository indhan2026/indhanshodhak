What IndhanShodhak Does

Built for Bharat

Every feature designed for real India — rural roads, fuel shortages, 12 languages, and zero tolerance for black-market diversion.

Real-Time Fuel Availability
Know which pumps have petrol, diesel, or CNG before you leave home. No more wasted trips.
Verified Pump Network
Only authenticated, licensed pumps earn the green verification badge. Trust what you see.
Community Rewards
Report fuel status, earn points, climb the leaderboard. Your update helps thousands of commuters.
Crisis Mode Alerts
Four-level crisis system activates automatically during fuel shortages, guiding users to available stations.
12 Indian Languages
Hindi, Marathi, Bengali, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Gujarati, and more — IndhanShodhak speaks your language.
Government Oversight
Dedicated dashboard for officials to monitor fuel distribution trends and respond to regional shortages.
EV Charging Stations
Track EV charging slot availability alongside traditional fuel — one app for every vehicle type.
Anti Black-Market Shield
Intelligent monitoring helps detect fuel diversion patterns, protecting fair access for every citizen.
Crisis Response System

Always Ready. Never Caught Off Guard.

Normal
